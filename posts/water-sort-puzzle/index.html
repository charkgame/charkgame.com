Water Sort

Water Sort is an innovative puzzle game that combines color-matching mechanics with strategic thinking. Players must sort different colored liquids between glasses to achieve uniform colors, offering both a mental challenge and a relaxing experience.

Game Mechanics

  • Basic Concept: Sort colored liquid segments into matching glasses
  • Color Matching: Pour only when colors match
  • Space Management: Ensure sufficient space in the receiving glass
  • Strategic Planning: Think ahead to avoid dead ends

Key Features

  • Simple Controls:
    • One-tap pouring mechanism
    • Intuitive glass selection
    • Easy-to-understand color matching
  • Gameplay Elements:
    • Progressive difficulty levels
    • Multiple glass configurations
    • Various color combinations
    • Restart option for challenging levels
