Watermelon Drop

Wednesday, Sep 17, 2025 | 1 minute read

@ yeqwep

Overview

  • Game Type: Merge puzzle game in the popular Suika genre.
  • Objective: Drop various fruits and vegetables into a cup to merge them.

Core Mechanics:

  • Merging: When two identical food items collide, they combine to form a larger, different food item.
  • Goal: Merge the largest and most fruits possible.
  • Game Over: The game ends when a fruit or vegetable falls over the edge of the cup due to lack of space.
  • Scoring: At the end of the game, your score is rated. The challenge is to achieve an “A” rank.

How to Play Watermelon Drop

  • Move: Use your mouse to move the fruit or vegetable.
  • Drop: Click to drop the item into the cup.
