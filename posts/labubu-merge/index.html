Labubu Merge

Monday, Sep 8, 2025 | 3 minute read

@ MoMo Games

Have you ever seen something so ugly it’s actually cute? Meet the Labubus ⎼ tiny plush monsters with mischievous smiles and sharp little teeth. In Labubu Merge, your task is simple: merge matching Labubus to unlock brand-new, even cuter (and weirder) plushies! Watch your collection grow as you discover endless combinations and expand your cuddly-creepy crew. Whether you’re just looking to unwind or chasing the thrill of creating the ultimate Labubu, this game has you covered. Be in trend. Merge the Labubus!

Game Overview

Similar to other merge games, Labubu Merge centers around the simple yet endless mechanic of merging identical Labubu dolls on a board to create new, cuter, and sillier dolls. Progression in the game is relatively simple: players start out with the smallest Labubu and combine similar-looking dolls until they create bigger ones. Players can also follow the ladder to see what comes from merging two kinds of dolls, starting from the tiniest Labubus (with Blueberry being the smallest) all the way up to the larger ones, like Labubu Chef, Agent, or even Labubu Crimson.

The thrill in the game comes from the need to achieve a bigger, better character by merging your Labubu dolls. With each transformation unlocking a colorful doll and paired with the funky background soundtrack, this game is a delight. The simple yet strategic merge challenges players to think before they act, since filling the basket results in a game over.

How to Play Labubu Merge?

  • Grab a Labubu doll and drag it: click and hold the left mouse button.
  • Drop it: release the mouse button to place the doll.
  • Merge identical dolls, unlock bigger Labubus, and score points: when two identical dolls collide, they create a new, bigger and sillier one. The more merges you make, the higher your score will be.

Good to Know!

  • Beware of the big boom! A merge cascade is the most thrilling part of the game, since when you’re least expecting it, there will be a big boom, resulting in the instant merge of several dolls at once. These chain reactions are visually satisfying and display flashy effects that add to the overall excitement. At the same time, they can get in your way and collide with smaller ones, making it less likely to merge what is below them.
  • Follow the evolution ladder: use it as a guide to see what comes next (e.g., two Rockstar Girls merge into a Wizardman).
  • Push left, push right! You have a limited number of helpful pushes, so when everything else you’ve tried hasn’t worked, you’ll still be able to try merging your dolls… but use them wisely!

Tips & Tricks

  • Clear board, clear mind! The best way to go far is to keep your board tidy. Don’t just throw dolls around ⎼ random drops will only fill up space and make merging harder.
  • Focus on rare Labubus: instead of merging every doll you see, pay extra attention to the rare ones. Place them smartly, push them together, and aim for big merges. That’s how you unlock the really powerful blends!
  • Trigger chain reactions! Want that big dopamine rush? Try triggering chain reactions. Not only does this free up your board, but it also gives you a massive score boost. Plan ahead, look for matches, and let the chains do the work!
